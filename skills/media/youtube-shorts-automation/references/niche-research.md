## 🏆 Top 5 Niches Ranked for Automation

### 1. 🥇 Psychology / Human Behavior
**Why**: Highest retention (78%), 2-4 months to monetize, endless content angles
**Automation difficulty**: Very Easy — universal relatability, templates work across topics
**Sub-niches**: Dark psychology, relationship psychology, success psychology, cognitive biases, social psychology
**Example hooks**: "Nunca debes hacer X en una discusión", "La gente exitosa tiene este hábito"

### 2. 🥈 "What If" Scenarios
**Why**: 2-3 months to monetize, near-zero research, infinite ideas
**Automation difficulty**: Trivial — just pick a scenario, speculate, no fact-checking needed
**Example hooks**: "¿Qué pasaría si la Tierra dejara de girar?", "¿Qué pasaría si los humanos vivieran 200 años?"
**Note**: Lower RPM but production speed makes up for it (5-7 videos in time of 1 finance video)

### 3. 🥉 AI News / Tool Reviews
**Why**: Highest RPM ($10-22), infinite content pipeline (new tools weekly), affiliate revenue
**Automation difficulty**: Medium — needs screen recordings, up-to-date info
**Best for**: Affiliate + sponsor revenue, not just AdSense

### 4. Dark History / Unsolved Mysteries
**Why**: 70-82% retention, evergreen content (cases stay relevant for years)
**Automation difficulty**: Medium — requires research, archive footage
**Best for**: Long-form (15-30 min), passive income from back catalog

### 5. Science Facts / Explainers
**Why**: Evergreen, global demand, public domain footage (NASA, ESA)
**Automation difficulty**: Low — facts are well-documented, images available

| Meditation / sleep | high | slow | Massively saturated, glacial growth |

## 📋 YouTube Monetization Requirements (2026)

**YPP Gate** (either):
- 1,000 subs + 4,000 watch hours (long-form) — OR —
- 1,000 subs + 10M Shorts views in 90 days

**Revenue split**: 45% creator, 55% YouTube (for Shorts ad pool)
