## Key Industry Trends (2026)

- **Nearshoring**: LatAm benefiting from supply chain diversification away from Asia
- **Technology adoption**: WMS, TMS, IoT tracking becoming standard
- **Sustainability**: "Green logistics" is a differentiator (DSV, others offering carbon-neutral options)
- **E-commerce specialization**: 3PLs developing dedicated e-comm fulfillment (pick/pack/ship/returns)
- **Consolidation**: Large players acquiring regional specialists
