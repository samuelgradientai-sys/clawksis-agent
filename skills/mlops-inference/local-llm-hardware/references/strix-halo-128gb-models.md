## How to verify RAM type from product page

Look for these phrases in specs:

| Phrase | Means | Expandable? |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| "Onboard LPDDR5X (non-upgradeable)" | Soldered | ❌ NO |
| "memory chips are soldered to the motherboard" | Soldered | ❌ NO |
| "LPDDR5x" | Soldered low-power | ❌ NO |
| "DDR5 SO-DIMM slots" | Socketed | ✅ YES |
| "2x DDR5 SODIMM" | Socketed, user-replaceable | ✅ YES |
| "4x DDR5 slots" | Socketed | ✅ YES |
